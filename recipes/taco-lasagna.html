Taco Lasagna

A beautiful new design for a taco. Giving more than just a few bites, while holding on to the wondeful flavor!

This recipe is a new take on a taco. It is meant to give you the same flavor and crunchiness, while letting you have bigger portions of this heavenly food, The juicy meat mixed with the melted cheese create a unique contrast to the tortilla chips and makes for a recipe that will change your world!

Ingredients:

  • Tortilla chips
  • Ground beef
  • Taco seasoning
  • Colby-Jack cheese
  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • Cilantro
  • Sour cream
  • Shredded lettuce
Steps:
  1. Add taco seasoning to the ground beef, then add some onion, and cilantro.
  2. Cook meat until evenly brown then drain the oil.
  3. In a dish add a layer of tortilla chips. Then a layer on meat and shredded cheese.
  4. Repeat that step until the dish is full.
  5. Preheat oven to 350F then bake the dish for a few minutes until cheese is melted.
  6. Take dish out of the oven and add sour cream, tomatoes and lettuce on top.
