Cheesecake

A smooth and creamy cheesecake with strawberry jam

This cheesecake is a simple homemade recipe. It was created using multiple tests to focus on the perfect texture. while it is a mix of sweetness and crunchy with a slight hint of fruity added by the strawberry jam, it still has a subtle flavor so you can eat more than just one slice.

Ingredients:

  • Graham crackers - 12 sheets or 1.25 cups
  • Melted butter - 5 table spoons
  • Granulated sugar - 1/4 cup
  • Cream cheese - 1 brick
  • Sweetend condensed milk - 1 can
  • Pineapple juice - 1 tablespoon
  • Vanilla extract - 1 teaspoon
  • Strawberry jam
  • Strawberries
Steps for the crust:
  1. Crush the crackers - You can use a food processor, blender, or put them in a ziploc bag and use a rolling pin.
  2. Mix the sugar - Add the sugar and the crumbs to a bowl and stir.
  3. Butter time - Add the melted butter to the bowl and stir it with a fork
  4. Check texture - Make sure everything is combined and lathered in butter.
  5. Press into the pan - Press the mixture into a pan so that it is tight against the bottom and sides then use your fingers to create an even edge
  6. Bake the crust - Bake at 350F for 10-13 minutes then allow to cool before adding filling.
Steps for filling:
  1. Cream cheese - Add it to the bowl and get ready to beat the ingredients together
  2. Pour the fun stuff in - Add the sweetend condensed milk, pineapple juice, and vanilla extract into the bowl
  3. Beat it up! - Beat the ingredients together until most if not all of the cream cheese clumps are dissolved.
  4. Fill it up - Pour the filling into the pie crust
  5. Pre-heat the oven - 325F
  6. Baking Time! - once the oven is preheated bake the cheesecake for 20-25 minutes
  7. Checking the cheesecake - To check if it is ready tap the side gently, if the center jiggles a little it is complete
  8. (optional) Open the oven door after you turn it off, then leave the cheesecake for an hour so that is cools down gradually preventing cracks.
  9. Cool down - Take the cheesecake out and let it cool to room temperature
  10. Add strawberry jam and strawberries on top - this is up to your discretion
