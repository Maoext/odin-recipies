Carne Asada Torta

A torta filled with Juicy meat, mouth-watering guac and other ingredients, covered in a crispy white bread.

The torta is a traditional mexican food that uses a crusty white bread roll also knows as a "bolillo" Which is used to enhance the flavor of the sandwich due to its crunchy exterior and soft inside. Many types of meat and ingredients are then added to enhance and change the flavor. The following recipe is one of my favorites due to its simplicity and flavor pallate.

Ingredients:

  • White bread roll "Bolillo"
  • Butter or mayo
  • Skirt steak beef
  • Marinade of choice
  • Salt and pepper
  • Guacamole or avocado
  • Jalapeno
  • Red onion
  • Quesadilla cheese
  • Cacique Crema Mexicana
Steps for the carne asada:
  1. Put steak and marinade together in a ziploc bag or in a pan and cover it.
  2. Put the combination into the fridge for at least 3 hours, but up to 12 hours.
  3. Add salt and pepper then grill steak until fully cooked.
  4. Chop the meat into strips or small squares and put it to the side.
Steps for the Torta:
  1. Cut the bread roll in half.
  2. Spread butter or mayo on the inside of the bread and toast it.
  3. After it gets to the desired toated level add a thin strip of cheese to the upper half of the bread.
  4. Cut the onions, jalapenos, tomatoes. and avocados if you chose to use them instead of guac.
  5. Add the deired amount of vegetables and cream and close it up.
  6. Enjoy!
